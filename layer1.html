The Physical Layer

The Physical Layer is for transmitting data(bits) over a physical medium. It is the actual physical connection from one device to another device. Physical Layer devices include:

  • Cables
  • Modems
  • Hubs

The Physical Layer determines topology, or how a network is organized. The type of signal used to transmit bits are also determined in Layer 1. Data Transfer Rate, known as bit rate is determined by the Physical Layer. The Physical Layer gives a foundation to the OSI by using physical devices to transmit datato begin the networking process.
